How strongly correlated are the features?

  • overall weak correlation between most of the features
  • strongest correlation between points and assists, and points/assists with outcome
  • points, assists, blocks and rebounds are positively correlated with the corresponding team's outcome (negatively correlated with opponent team's outcome)
  • turnovers and personal fouls are negatively correlated with the corresponding team's outcome (positively correlated with opponent team's outcome)
Individually, the features may not be strongly associated with the game outcome, but the combination of all of the features may have a significant impact on who comes out as the winner.
